Our Story

A Senior's Legacy & a Housewife's Passion

Yi Oasis began with two dreams. Uncle Chua wished to create a lasting legacy for his grandchildren, while Elaine longed to start a business venture of her own. While away on holiday together, both dreams merged — and Yi Oasis was born.

Today, Yi Oasis is more than just a business. It is a testament to friendship, family, and the pursuit of dreams — built on the belief that everyone deserves a moment of true, indulgent relaxation.

"Just Live Your Dreams." ✨

Building a legacy for future generations. Fostering strong bonds. Embracing passions and turning them into reality.

Every treatment at Yi Oasis is delivered with that same spirit — care, warmth, and genuine indulgence for every guest who walks through our doors.
